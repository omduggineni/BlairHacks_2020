Race to Space!

Easy Medium Hard

Multiplayer

Easy Medium Hard

Controls: W to go up, A to go left, S to go down, D to go right.
In this game, you control a rocket as it goes through the earth's atmosphere and space. You will have to dodge asteroids, and successfully dock your satellite on the International Space Station. You first start by launching your rocket as it flies through the atmosphere, shown by the successively darker shades of blue. Once you get to the black background, you will have to dodge asteroids. You will have a health bar shown on the bottom. Finally, once you get to the screen with the space station, you wil have to dock your rocket on the green rectngle. Be careful however, you can't speed too fast into the space station and you only have a set amount of fuel. You also lose health bars from bumping into walls. Good Luck!
